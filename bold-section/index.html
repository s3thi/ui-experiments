Rocko's Modern Life

Rocko lives in O-Town. Raised in Australia, Rocko later emigrated to the United States after he graduated high school. Murray suggests that Rocko's interest in the United States began with a vacation with his family as a youth; during the trip he met his future sidekick Filburt.

Inconsistencies

Murray allowed for directors to create inconsistencies, reflecting how friends possess different and varied, nostalgic memories of the past.

Fan Mail

For instance, in the episode "Put to Pasture" the writers portray Rocko, Filburt and Heffer Wolfe as students at O-Town High School. Murray received many pieces of mail regarding the storyline inconsistencies.
